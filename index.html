Kevin Rao

I'm Kevin, a senior at Harvard studying Computer Science. I'm very fortunate to be advised by the wonderful Boaz Barak, and as of Fall 2021 I'm applying to CS Ph.D. programs.

I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science, and I look forward to the day when I have enough experience to be more certain and specific with my preferences. At the moment, I'm working on problems in cryptography and circuit complexity.
