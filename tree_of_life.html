Tree of Life

One of my hobbies is learning about nature and the evolutionary relationship between species. I started this personal project as a tool to help record interesting facts as I learn them, and over time the tree has kept growing as I keep adding to it. When the tree has gotten too deep and is continued on a different pdf, this is denoted with the "∈" symbol.

The actual version is in Overleaf and this page is updated by periodic pdf uploads.
